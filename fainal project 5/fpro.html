About Tabuk

Tabuk is a historic city in northwestern Saudi Arabia. It has played a significant role in trade, culture, and tourism. With proximity to NEOM and the Red Sea, it’s a gateway to natural and historical beauty.

  • Population: 970,000 (2024)
  • Known for: Al Disah Valley, Tabuk Castle, NEOM region
