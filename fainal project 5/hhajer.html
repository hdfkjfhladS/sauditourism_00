Mountain Bride

Abha is one of the most beautiful and distinctive tourist destinations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Located in the Asir region in the southwest of the Kingdom, Abha is a city with diverse terrain and a rich culture, making it one of the most prominent domestic tourist destinations in the Kingdom.

Farfalle Restaurant

Farfalle Italian Restaurant is highly regarded among other restaurants, making it one of the best restaurants in Abha. This is perhaps due to the delicious food it serves, which has been highly appreciated by most guests. It offers a diverse menu of pizza, pasta, and salads, including: chicken lasagna, fettuccine, chef's pizza, spinach pizza, Mista salad, and Italian tomato soup.

Narang Restaurant

One of the beautiful and clean restaurants, which offers open seating on the ground floor and private closed seating. The restaurant preserves the ancient folk heritage despite its luxury and modernity. Customers live a unique experience by tasting the most delicious foods. It is one of the most famous restaurants in Abha for families, as it diversifies its menus and lunch meals. You will find grilled dishes, rice, soups, and salads, in addition to a variety of juices, with meals specifically for children.
