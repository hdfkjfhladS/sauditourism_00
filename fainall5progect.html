About Riyadh

Riyadh is the capital and largest city of Saudi Arabia, known for its modern skyline, economic influence, and cultural landmarks. It is the heart of the Kingdom’s political and administrative affairs.

  • Population: 7.6 million (2024)
  • Known for: Kingdom Tower, Diriyah, Riyadh Season
